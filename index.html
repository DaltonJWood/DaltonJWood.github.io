Mount St. Helens

Mount St. Helens is a volcano located in Skamania County, Washington. It is most famous for its eruption in 1980.

The Eruption!

As mentioned above, Mount St. Helens erupted on May 17th, 1980! The eruption destroyed over 200 homes, more than 185 miles of roads as well as 15 miles of railways. The full estimated damage done is worth $1.1 billion.
